# Governance Considerations for RAG

RAG systems require strong governance to ensure responsible, secure, and compliant AI usage.

---

## 1. Data Governance
- Sensitive data filtering
- Access control
- Data retention policies
- Audit trails

---

## 2. Model Governance
- Embedding model selection
- LLM versioning
- Performance monitoring
- Drift detection

---

## 3. Retrieval Governance
- Relevance thresholds
- Document approval workflows
- Chunking strategy
- Retrieval explainability

---

## 4. Responsible AI
- Fairness
- Transparency
- Human oversight
- Risk mitigation

---

## 5. Compliance
- Legal review
- Regulatory alignment
- Privacy requirements
- Security controls

---

This ensures RAG systems are safe, trustworthy, and enterprise-ready.
